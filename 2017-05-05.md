## Notes and hints

- How do you say 'pernicious'?

1. There are three syllables: per-ni-cious
2. The stress is on the second syllable: perNIcious
3. The 'c' is pronounced /ʃ/
4. In phonemic script, the word is written like this: /pə(r)ˈnɪʃəs/
